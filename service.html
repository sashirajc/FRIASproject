  • Service

  • Delivery details

    • All our products are delivered within 4 working days of the order being placed. For orders placed on weekends or public holidays an additional delivery period of 2 days can be expected despite our best efforts to deliver your orders on time.

     

  • Free Delivery

    • For all orders worth S$50 or more we will deliver it at your door step free of charge(not applicable for international orders). For all local deliveries below S$50 a flat delivery charge of S$7 would be charged. For international deliveries standard courier charges apply.

     

  • Measurements

    • We have custom orders for people who wish to alter their clothes to fit them better. Our delivery personnel have all the tools required to customize the apparel to fit your size. Please note that designs, textures and colours on the apparel can not be customized by our personanel. All customization is restricted to physical size and shape.

     

  • Cash on Delivery

    • Cash on Delivery option is currently available for orders less than 2 items. For larger orders we would prefer to first make payment on paypal before the order is collected and the product is dispatched.

     

  • Product Returns

    • All our products can be returned within a period of 30 days from the day of receipt of the product. Products will be accepted only if there are no damages. Damages include tear on the fabric,stitching and/or any other accessories attached to the apparel. Returned products will be accepted only if there are no signs of use. Returns must be made with a return slip at a SingPost counter or POPStation. Undergarments once purchased can not be returned.
