Frias Kids Wear

24 Aug 2015

FRIAS is a Kids Wear fashion outlet that aims to provide the highest quality, and most comfortable clothes for kids of all ages with our value-added services such as full body measurements to allow kids to have the best fit.

 

Our vision is to inspire the kids' world. Our mission is to provide a complete experience for todays’ kids with exciting fashion lifestyle products.

 

Frias online kids’ clothing collections is created by our dedicated in-house team in Singapore, who draw inspiration from the kids in their lives as well as conduct a painstaking research on fashion trends around the globe. Our comprehensive range of collection offers you colourful clothes at affordable prices. You may rest assured as our in-house team focuses on the comfort of children while assuring softness and improved durability.

 

Our in-house team comprises moms who know what looks best on kids and what comfort means to the young ones. Therefore, we ensure that our clothes are soft as well as convenient for everyday wear. With our endless attention to detail, we make sure that your kids are dressed in an exclusive set of clothes that are pleasing to the eye as well as comfortable for wear.

 

With our strong mix of unique quality product, marketing expertise and customer focus, we offer you an exceptional brand. However, we attribute our growth and success to our dedicated team, their energy and meticulous effort.

 

Visit Frias now! Your children are sure to love the comfort of our unique range of clothes.
