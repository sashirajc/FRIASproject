Q:How do I place an order?

A:You can place an order by clicking on the payment tab on the top right corner of the webpage. Alternatively you can also call us at +65-44446677 or drop by our store at Orchard Crescent to try out our products before purchasing. Our friendly staff will assist you in finding the best apparel to suit your children's style.

 

Q:Can I change/cancel my order after it is placed?

A:You may change/cancel your order as long as the product has not been dispatched. You can call our customer care service at +65-44446677 to change/cancel your orders.

 

Q:Do you offer gift wrapping services?

A:Our gift wrapping services are only available at our store at Orchard Crescent. For online orders we do not have gift wrapping services at the moment.

 

Q:What is your shipping policy?

A:At FRIAS we try to ship your products within 2 working days. But depending on the availability of the products at our warehouse it might take upto 4 days for your orders to reach you.

 

Q:What is your return policy?

A:If you are not satisfied with your order then you may return the product within a period of 30 days to qualify for a full refund.Returns must be made with a return slip at a SingPost counter or POPStation. Products must be ubused with tags on, in original packaging and without any damage. Undergarments can not be returned due to hygiene concerns.

 

Q:Do I have to pay for return shipping?

A:We believe that return shipping should be free for our valued customers. All our products are shipped with pre-paid return tags. In case of retuns kindly use the pre-paid tags to return the product to us. In case the tag is missing kindly call our customer care service and we would assist you with alternative return options.

 

Q:How will I be refunded?

A:For credit/debit card payments, the money would be transferred to your card. For all COD payments refunds would be made through bank transfer to the account number provided on the pre-paid post tag.

 

Q:How long will it take for me to get my refund?

A:The returned product takes 3-5 days to reach our warehouse. Once we receive your returned product it would take 1-2 working days for us to refund your money.

 

Q:Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

A:Every credit card transaction occurs within a secure environment. Our payment system has a 128-bit SSL security encryption certification awarded by VeriSign. You can see the transaction is secure if you see a keylock at the bottom right of your web browser.

 

Q:Would my credit card details be stored on your website?

A:We do not retain your credit card information after your order is completed. Rather, it is submitted directly to our banks. You can rest assured that with each purchase your credit card or bank account information will be secured.

 

Q:Will my personal details be sold to third parties?

A:Your privacy and your trust are important to us. Your information and details will not be sold or passed to any third party.

 

Q:Is the data I share when I make a purchase secure?

A:We value and understand the importance of your privacy. We work hard to ensure that your personal details are secure and are not released voluntarily, or without your permission, to a third party organisation.

 

Q:Does your cafe serve alcoholic beverages?

A:Our cafe will open soon and you can visit our cafe. We dont want to spoil the surprise for you.

 

Q:What are the working hours of your store?

A:Our store at Orchard Crescent works from 10AM-10PM. Our online shop is open 24x7. Our telephone orders are functional from 11AM-9PM only.

 

Q:When are your fashion shows taking place?

A:For information regarding our fashion shows kindly check the events page.

 

Q:Is your fashion show aimed at children?

A:Our fashion shows are aimed at all age groups. We have separate fashion shows for children and for adults.

 

Q:Are children allowed for adult fashion shows?

A:Children below the age of 16 are only allowed for children fashion shows. Children between the ages of 16-19 are allowed if accompanied by an adult.
